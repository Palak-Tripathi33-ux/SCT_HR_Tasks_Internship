📁 Projects & Tasks Breakdown

Task 1 — Job Description: Data Analyst

Formulated a comprehensive, ATS-optimized Job Description for a Mid-Level Data Analyst role targeting the Business Intelligence department.

Defined salary benchmarks (₹7,00,000–₹10,00,000 per annum) aligned with Bangalore market standards.

Outlined 4 key responsibilities: Data Collection & Cleaning, Reporting & Dashboards (Power BI/Tableau), Cross-Functional Collaboration, and KPI Monitoring.

Specified 3 technical skill requirements (Advanced Excel, SQL, Data Visualization) and 3 soft skill requirements (Communication, Analytical Thinking, Time Management).

Structured a complete perks & benefits section covering EPF, health insurance, paid certifications (Coursera/Udemy), and hybrid work model.

Task 2 — Remote Intern Onboarding Plan (30 Days)

Designed a structured 30-day remote onboarding plan to integrate new interns smoothly from Day 1.

Developed a week-by-week phased framework across 4 stages: Learn & Adapt → Shadow & Practice → Contribute & Create → Execute & Review.

Built an IT & Access Checklist covering 3 key areas: Communication Tools (Google Workspace, Slack/Teams), Project Management Access (Trello/Jira/Asana), and Security Setup (VPN, 1Password/LastPass).

Defined a 4-role Team Introduction Plan assigning clear responsibilities to the HR Manager, Mentor/Buddy, Project Manager, and Team Members.

Set measurable weekly milestones: 100% onboarding session attendance in Week 1, 2 practice assignments completed by Week 2, first live task delivered in Week 3.

Task 3 — Performance Appraisal Report (Employee: Rahul Sharma, Q2 2026)

Conducted a mock performance appraisal for a Junior Developer (Software Development Department) against a 5-point rating scale.

Evaluated 4 performance criteria: Technical Skills (5/5 — Outstanding), Work Productivity & Deadlines (4/5 — Exceeds), Communication Skills (2/5 — Needs Improvement), Teamwork & Collaboration (3/5 — Meets).

Authored structured HR evaluation feedback identifying communication gaps and cross-functional collaboration challenges.

Developed a 3-KPI Improvement Plan:

KPI 1 — Active Presentation: Deliver 2 internal technical demos within 90 days.

KPI 2 — Daily Reporting Accuracy: Achieve 100% complete daily updates on Jira/Slack.

KPI 3 — Professional Training: Complete a Business Communication & Stakeholder Management course on Coursera within 30 days.

Task 4 — Employee Retention Strategy Report

Researched and documented 8 major causes of high employee turnover in the tech industry including burnout, limited career growth, poor work-life balance, and remote work isolation.

Formulated a 3-pillar Strategic Retention Plan targeting mental health, career development, and team engagement.

Pillar 1 — Mental Health & Well-Being: Proposed 6 initiatives including monthly wellness sessions, No-Meeting Fridays, a confidential Employee Assistance Program (EAP), paid mental health leave, and flexible working hours.

Pillar 2 — Career Progression Framework: Designed 6 initiatives including transparent promotion roadmaps, quarterly performance reviews, mentorship programs for junior employees, and a dedicated L&D budget.

Pillar 3 — Virtual Team-Building: Introduced weekly non-work video sessions, online gaming competitions, and monthly cross-team coffee chats to combat remote isolation.

Built a 6-month phased Implementation Roadmap with ownership allocation across HR Operations, L&D, Engineering, and Culture Committee.

Defined 5 measurable Key Retention Metrics with targets: Attrition Rate (15% → below 8%), Employee Satisfaction Score (65% → above 85%), Internal Promotion Rate (10% → above 20%), Training Participation (40% → above 75%).
